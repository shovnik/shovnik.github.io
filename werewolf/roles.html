Roles

Ability Snatchers

Every night, you may curse a different player of your choice. The cursed player may not use any ability until the end of the next night.
When a player is announced dead, you may opt to reveal your role, abandon it and assume the role of the dead player.
Every night, you may assume the role of the player of your choice leaving the player with the role of the thief instead.

Life Manipulators

Every night, you may choose to heal a different player. If the healed player was attacked, the attack is negated.
In the moment before you die, you may reveal your role to shoot and kill a player of your choice.
Every night, all remaining siblings meet for a brief silent exchange to execute one other player over the course of the game provided their decision is unanimous.
If you are attacked, you are announced fatally wounded the next morning. After a player has been lynched, you perish.
Every night, you discover which players the wolves have attacked. You possess one healing potion and one killing potion to use on players over the course of the game, but you do not know which is which.

Detection Speciliasts

Every night, the growl of the bear resonates through the village if at least one player of a different allegiance group is beside you.
Every night, you may use your flair to check if at least one of three consecutive players of your choice is a wolf. If you do not find one, your flair is lost forever.
Every night, you may compare the aura of two players of your choice. It will be revealed to you if they share the same allegiance group.
Every night, you may see in your crystal ball if a player of your choice is a wolf or not.

Lynch Meddlers

Every night, you may designate a player of your choice. When tallying the votes for the lynching, the total vote count for the designated player is doubled.
If you are designated to be lynched, your role is revealed to the village. Feeling foolish for having suspected you of such devious crimes, the villagers decide to spare you, but you lose your right to vote for the rest of the game.
Over the course of the game, after a player has been lynched, you may once reveal your role to initiate a new lynching immediately.
Every night, you may designate a player of your choice. When tallying the votes for the lynching, the total vote count for the designated player is halved.
