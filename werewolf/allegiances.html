Allegiances

Villagers

Eradicate all wolves from the village.

If you are lynched, your allegiance is revealed to the village, and all remaining players lose the abilities associated with their role.
Every night, you may spend the night with the player of your choice. You get killed if your lover is attacked rather than if you yourself are attacked. Careful, if you sleep with a wolf, the wolf devours you.
When you die, you may give your role to the player of your choice. In this scenario, you act before the pupil.
There is nothing special about you. You basically just wake up in the morning, toil away, lynch a poor chap, and pray not to be devoured at night.
Only on the first night, you get to meet your soulmate shrouded in silence and fall hopelessly in love. There onwards, if one of you may perish, the other follows. How tragic!

Deranged

Trick the village into lynching you.

There is nothing in the world you fantasize more than getting lynched. The intense feeling as the rope tightens around your neck but a moment away from eternal silence. As thanks for lynching you, after you die, you may give one of the lucky few who voted for your lynching the sweet release of death.

Werewolves

Devour villagers until only the wolf pack remains.

You have a big bad appetite. Starting from the second night, you wake up an additional time when you may devour a second victim as a midnight snack.
If you die, furious that their little cub was killed, the wolves get two kills the next night.
You live on a different time zone always doing things at your own pace. You awaken alone after the pack. You may then elect to shift the wolves’ victim for the night one player clockwise or anti-clockwise.
There is nothing special about you. You basically just wake up in the morning, toil away, lynch a poor chap, and furiously devour someone at night.

Outcasts

Deceive the village until you remain the lone survivor.

You wake and act with the wolves as one of them. However, starting from the third night, you awaken an additional time when you may kill a fellow wolf in cold blood.
Sometimes you wish you had a better reason for doing this, but the truth is that there is simply nothing you crave more than a juicy soul. At the start of every night following the first, the souls of the dead players visit you. You may consume the one of your choice and gain its abilities for the rest of the game.
