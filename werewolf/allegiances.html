Allegiances

Villagers

Eradicate all werewolves and outcasts.

If you are lynched, your allegiance is revealed to the village, and all remaining players lose the abilities associated with their roles.
Every night, you may designate a player of your choice as your lover. You get killed if your lover is attacked rather than if you yourself are attacked, however if your lover is a wolf, your lover devours you.
When you die, you may give your role accompanied with any ability associated with it to the player of your choice. In this scenario, you act before the pupil.
You are a common villager. Work with your fellow villagers to lynch all those who oppose the village.
You get to meet your soulmate the first night shrouded in silence falling hopelessly in love. There onwards, if one of you may perish, the other follows.

Deranged

Trick the village into lynching you.

There is nothing in you fantasize more than getting lynched. The intense feeling as the rope tightens around your neck but a moment away from eternal peace. As thanks, after you die, you may give one of those who voted to lynch you the sweet release of death.

Werewolves

Devour villagers until only the wolf pack remains.

Starting from the second night, you wake an additional time when you may devour a second victim of your choice.
If you die, furious by the death of their cub, the wolves attack an extra villager the following night.
You only wake alone after the pack and your identity remains unknown to the other wolves. You may elect to shift which victim the wolves shall attack for the night one player clockwise or anti-clockwise.
You are a common wolf. Work with your fellow wolves to eliminate all those who oppose the pack.

Outcasts

Deceive the village to become the lone survivor.

You wake and act with the wolves as one of them. Starting from the third night, you awaken an additional time when you may kill a fellow wolf in cold blood.
The souls of the dead players visit you every night for you to feast upon. You gain the abilities associated with the role of all the souls you consume for the rest of the game.
