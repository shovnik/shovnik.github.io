Games

2048

How to play:

Slide numbered tiles on grid combining those of same value to create tile with the number 2048. However, you may continue thereafter, creating tiles with even larger numbers.

Controls:

  • Arrow keys slide the tiles in the direction pressed

Minesweeper

How to play:

Clear minefield by revealing cells and marking mine locations without detonating any mines by using surrounding mine indices that appear on revealed cells.

Controls:

  • Left click hidden cell to reveal
  • Right click hidden cell to mark as mined
  • Click revealed cell with correct amount of surrounding marked cells to reveal surrounding hidden cells

Slide Puzzle

How to play:

Order tiles from top left corner to bottom right corner of grid by increasing value in as little time or as few moves as possible by sliding tiles into the vacant space.

Controls:

  • Click tile adjacent to vacant space to slide it in

Snake

How to play:

Make the snake grow as large as possible by eating "food" without colliding with any of the walls or the snake's own tail.

Controls:

  • Arrow keys change direction snake is heading

Tetris

How to play:

Score as high a score as possible clearing rows by filling them with random tetronimo shaped blocks that continuously fall off the top of the screen without overflowing out of the arena.

Controls:

  • Left and right arrow keys shift block side to side
  • Down arrow key drops block faster
  • Up arrow and control key rotate blocks clockwise and counter-clockwise respectively
  • Shift key stores block for future use
  • Spacebar instantly drops block until it collides
